Call for Papers

The organizers of the National Conference on Next-Generation Technologies in AI Applications warmly invite esteemed researchers, academicians, industry experts, professionals, and students to contribute original research results, case studies, and technical insights that explore emerging trends, innovative solutions, and contemporary challenges in digital technologies.

This conference offers a dynamic, interdisciplinary platform to exchange knowledge, inspire innovation, and foster collaborative research at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Computing Systems. It aims to showcase recent innovations, technological advancements, and research in the ever-evolving domains of AI, Big Data, and Emerging Technologies. All accepted abstracts will be peer-reviewed and considered for presentation and possible publication in conference proceedings or special issues of scientific journals. Authors will be informed about acceptance of their full papers based on the recommendations of the peer review committee.

Paper Submission Guidelines and Publications

Authors are requested to submit original papers as per IEEE format (not published or presented earlier) in Word/PDF format only. Each paper must be no more than 6(+2) pages (including abstract, figures, tables, and references). Extra pages will be charged at ₹250 per extra page. Final abstracts and research articles should be emailed to ncntabc2025@gmail.com.

Plagiarism is the wrongful appropriation of another author's language, thoughts, ideas, or expressions and representing them as one's own. Please check your content through plagiarism software, have your paper reviewed by experts, and ensure originality.

The proceedings containing abstracts of all accepted papers will be released on the first day of NCNTAIA-2025. Selected papers will be peer-reviewed by experts and published free of charge in Scopus/WoS indexed journals.

Submission Guidelines

  • Manuscripts should be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
  • All submissions will undergo double-blind peer review by an expert committee.
  • Accepted papers will be presented at the conference and published in the official Conference Proceedings.
  • Selected high-quality papers will be considered for publication in Scopus/WoS indexed journals.
