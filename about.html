About SNIST

SREENIDHI INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY (SNIST), established in 1997 and affiliated to JNTUH, transforms its students into competent, communicative, and application-oriented engineers ready for global challenges.

  • Accredited by NAAC with 'A' Grade and NBA
  • Received World Bank assistance under TEQIP
  • Active R&D in Nano Technology
  • Recognized by DSIR as a Scientific Research Organization
  • Numerous accolades for HRD practices, best college awards, and faculty research

About CSE Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at SNIST, established in 1997, offers both UG and PG programs with a focus on academic excellence, research, and industry engagement.

  • Offers B.Tech (CSE) and M.Tech with AI, ML, DS, Cyber Security, and IoT specializations
  • 15+ PhD faculty, active in patents and publications
  • Exclusive research labs and NPTEL support
  • Highest placement of ₹38 LPA; average ₹7.1 LPA
  • Students engaged in hackathons, sports, and co-curricular excellence
