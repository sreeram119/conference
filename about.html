About NCNTAIA-2025

In an age of accelerating digital transformation, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Big Data Analytics, and Smart Computing are reshaping the global landscape across academics, industries, governance, and everyday life. The National Conference on Next-Generation Technologies in AI Applications (NCNTAIA-2025) is an important initiative to facilitate the exchange of cutting-edge research, breakthrough innovations, and impactful practices that define this new technological epoch.

This academic and industry-oriented conference seeks to bring together a diverse community of scholars, scientists, developers, educators, entrepreneurs, and policymakers to explore advancements that are fueling next-generation systems. With a strong focus on interdisciplinary collaboration, the event provides a dynamic forum for the presentation of high-quality research results, the demonstration of real-world applications, and the discussion of strategies for responsible and scalable tech integration.

Keynote presentations by renowned experts in computing and technologies provide an exceptional opportunity for all registered participants to engage with domain experts, explore groundbreaking ideas, and stay ahead in the evolving world of technology. To recognize excellence in research, Best Paper Awards will be presented to the most impactful and innovative solution-oriented outcomes.

Objectives of the Conference

  • Promote Research and Innovation:
    • Provide a dynamic platform for sharing innovative research, emerging trends, and practical applications in AI, Big Data Analytics, and Smart Computing.
    • Empower emerging researchers and innovators—students, young researchers, and start-ups—to showcase their work, engage with experts, and gain mentorship and collaboration opportunities.
  • Foster Collaboration: Encourage collaboration among researchers, industry experts, and academicians for interdisciplinary projects and knowledge sharing in AI, Big Data, and Smart Computing.
  • Promote Sustainable Technological Solutions: Address how AI technologies can be leveraged for sustainable development—solving challenges such as climate change, public health, and economic inequality—while ensuring digital solutions are ethically responsible and socially inclusive.
  • Address National Challenges through Technology: Bring global perspectives to discuss national challenges and opportunities using AI and Big Data for innovation, policy, and technological equity.

Expected Highlights

  • Keynote presentations by renowned experts in computing and technology.
  • Paper presentations from contributors around the globe.
  • Panel discussions on the future of AI and innovation in various applications.
  • Networking opportunities with scholars and professionals.
  • Best Paper Awards for papers demonstrating significant innovation, originality, and research impact.
